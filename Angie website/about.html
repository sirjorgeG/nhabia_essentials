About NhaBia Essentials

Nhäbiä is inspired by my beloved grandmother— my "mother" in every sense of the word. By naming my business after her, I wanted to honour her legacy and the love she instilled in me.

Born during the lockdown, Nhäbiä started as a simple idea that quickly blossomed into a true passion.

At Nhäbiä, my mission is to celebrate the beauty of simplicity. I believe the best skincare comes from pure, uncomplicated ingredients that work in harmony with your skin.

Every product is handcrafted in small batches, ensuring you receive only the highest quality and care.

Join me on this journey of love, heritage and natural beauty.
